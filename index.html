How This Scheduler Works

Preemptive Priority Scheduling

  • Processes are executed based on priority (lower number = higher priority)
  • Scheduler can preempt a running process if a higher priority process arrives
  • Normally can lead to starvation of low-priority processes

Aging Mechanism

  • Gradually increases priority of waiting processes over time
  • Prevents starvation by ensuring all processes eventually get CPU time
  • Aging rate controls how quickly priorities increase
